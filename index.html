About Me

I graduated from Civil Aviation University of China in 2016 with a bachelor's degree in Electronic Information Engineering. After graduation, I worked as a software developer and later became an image algorithm engineer.

I developed many projects in C/C++, Python and JavaScript, for more than 50,000 lines, so I'm familiar with these programming languages and software design patterns. I'm good at traditional image processing algorithms, such as filtering, morphological operations, measurement of image properties, geometric transforms and feature description. In the field of deep learning, I have a good understanding of various neural network models, loss functions, training methods and evaluation indicators. I have also developed some related projects, such as pulmonary nodule detection, intracranial hemorrhage classification, image style transfer.
